Executive Summary

Comprehensive ERP Solution for Import-Distribution Operations

The Challenge

Import-distribution businesses face significant operational challenges: manual inventory tracking leading to stockouts and overstocking, fragmented purchase order management across multiple suppliers, lack of real-time visibility across warehouses, inefficient credit and recovery management, and time-consuming manual reporting. These inefficiencies result in lost revenue opportunities, poor cash flow, and inability to scale operations effectively.

The Solution

Hisham Traders ERP is a comprehensive, purpose-built management platform designed specifically for import-distribution businesses. The system provides end-to-end digitalization from procurement to payment collection, with industry-specific features that address your unique operational needs.

Core Capabilities:
  • Multi-warehouse inventory management with real-time tracking and batch/lot traceability
  • Import documentation tracking with automated landed cost calculation
  • Sales management with automated stock deduction and credit limit enforcement
  • Intelligent recovery scheduling with aging analysis and automated reminders
  • Comprehensive reporting suite with Excel export across all modules

System Modules & Features

Comprehensive functionality across all business operations

1. Foundation & Authentication

Secure access control and user management

  • • 5 role-based access levels (Admin, Warehouse Manager, Sales Officer, Accountant, Recovery Agent)
  • • Complete audit trail from Day 1 - track every change and transaction
  • • Granular permission control for sensitive operations

2. Import & Inventory Management

End-to-end procurement and inventory control

  • • Purchase order management with container tracking and supplier management
  • • Automated landed cost calculation (product cost + customs + taxes + shipping)
  • • Multi-warehouse inventory with bin location and batch/lot tracking
  • • Stock receiving, adjustments, and transfers with full audit trail
  • • Low stock alerts and reorder point management

3. Sales & Client Management

Streamlined sales processing and customer tracking

  • • Client database with credit limits, payment terms, and contact history
  • • Sales invoice creation with automatic stock deduction and pricing
  • • Real-time credit limit enforcement with warnings and approval workflows
  • • Payment recording for both clients and suppliers

4. Reports & Analytics

Comprehensive business intelligence and reporting

  • • Stock reports (current inventory, valuation, movement history)
  • • Sales and payment reports with date range filtering
  • • Import cost analysis and landed cost breakdown
  • • Excel export functionality across all reports
  • • Role-specific dashboards with real-time KPIs

Recovery Management: Aging analysis, weekly schedules, automated reminders
