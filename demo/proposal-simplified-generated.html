System Overview

The Challenge

Import-distribution businesses struggle with: manual inventory tracking causing stockouts, fragmented purchase order management, lack of real-time visibility, inefficient credit management, and time-consuming manual reporting.

A complete, purpose-built ERP system designed specifically for import-distribution operations. End-to-end digitalization from procurement to payment collection with industry-specific features.

System Modules

Phase 1: Core System (Base Package)

6-8 Week Delivery - Essential Operations

1. Foundation & Authentication
  • • 5 Role-based access (Admin, Warehouse, Sales, Accountant, Recovery)
  • • Complete audit trail from Day 1
  • • User management with permissions
  • • Role-specific dashboards
2. Import & Inventory
  • • Purchase orders with container tracking
  • • Landed cost calculation (product + customs + shipping)
  • • Multi-warehouse inventory management
  • • Stock receiving & adjustments with audit trail
3. Sales & Payments
  • • Client management with credit limits
  • • Sales invoices with auto stock deduction
  • • Credit limit enforcement & warnings
  • • Payment tracking (clients & suppliers)
4. Reports & Dashboards
  • • Stock reports (current, valuation, movements)
  • • Sales & payment reports
  • • Import cost analysis
  • • Excel export for all reports

Additional 4-6 Weeks - Advanced Operations

5. General Ledger & Accounting
  • • Double-entry accounting with Chart of Accounts
  • • Auto journal entries from transactions
  • • Trial balance & balance sheet
  • • Multi-bank account tracking
6. Advanced Inventory
  • • Gate pass system (auto/manual approval)
  • • Stock transfers between warehouses
  • • Bin location & batch tracking
  • • Physical stock count & cycle counting
7. Recovery Management
  • • Aging analysis & overdue tracking
  • • Weekly recovery schedules
  • • Payment reminders & follow-ups
  • • Collection agent assignment

  • • Advanced audit trail viewer with filters
  • • Change history comparison
  • • Compliance reporting
